Michael Eriksson has written a suite of calculators in Visual Basic. These calculate:
  • Chute size
  • Decent rate
  • Drift
  • Black Powder
  • Trust vs. Weight
Note that this is a stand-alone application which you download to your machine. Download it from his site URL: www.gbg.bonet.se/GME/INDEX/Sidor/Program.zip, size: 650Kb.
